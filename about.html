Our Story

Narobbina Safety & Security Solution has emerged as a leader in the security and safety industry in Ethiopia, combining local expertise with cutting-edge global technology. We are more than just a provider of security systems; we are a partner committed to protecting what matters most your people, assets, and business. Our approach is rooted in deep partnerships with leading global brands, most notably Hikvision, the world's leading manufacturer of video surveillance products. Our close collaboration with manufacturers allows us to bring the latest security technologies to Ethiopia, tailored to the unique needs of our clients.

As one of the few officially authorized agents and distributors of Hikvision in Ethiopia, we work hand in hand with producers to ensure that our clients receive nothing but the best—world-class products, expert advice, and flawless execution. Our commitment to customer satisfaction, quality, and continuous innovation has earned us a reputation for delivering comprehensive and reliable security solutions that keep pace with the evolving landscape of risks and threats.

Our Mission, Vision

OUR MISSION

Empowering Ethiopia's future with cutting-edge security solutions. At Narobbina, we blend global innovation with local expertise to safeguard what matters most—driving a safer, smarter, and more secure tomorrow.

OUR VISION

To create a safer, more secure Ethiopia where every individual and business feels protected and empowered through innovative, reliable, and cutting-edge security solutions.

Our Values

Customer Focused

Every client is unique, and so are their security needs. We focus on delivering highly personalized services, ensuring that every solution is customized to fit the specific requirements of each client, whether they are a small retail store or a large industrial complex.

Innovation

We are constantly exploring new technologies and solutions to stay ahead of the security curve. Innovation is at the core of everything we do, enabling us to provide our clients with state-of-the-art systems that evolve with their needs.

Trust & Integrity

Trust is the cornerstone of our business. We build long-term relationships with our clients based on integrity, transparency, and ethical practices. Our clients can rely on us to safeguard their most valuable assets, knowing that we hold their security interests above all else.

Excellence

Excellence is our goal in everything we do, from system design and product selection to customer service and after-sales support. We continually strive to exceed expectations by maintaining a high standard of service.
